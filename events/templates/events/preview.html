Education event November 28, 2023

A Value Session with Winners International School, Nkpor

On November the 28th, the RV team visited the founder's Alma Mater, Winners International School, Nkpor where values where taught to the children. The Headmistress/Principal dressed in purple gown was so pleased to see her fruits as priest and a first class Pharmacist. Her joy knew no bounds..

Education event September 01, 2023

A 30day Conversation with young people

The recreating values society gathered on Mondays from the 1st of September to the 30th to discuss pertinent values issues. With the belief that a society is sure of progress when it prepares for the future, the meet aimed to think of that future. The event was streamed live on www.facebook.com/Valentine.Obiagwu.recreating.values

Seminar event July 1, 2023

An RV Symposium on the role of creighton model fertility care system in napro technology

The Recreating values organization hosted its second ever symposium on the role of creighton model fertility care system in napro technology. Here, doctors and professionals in the medical and health sector talked about topics centered on better understanding fertility and management in the human system

Visitation event June 15, 2023

Outreach program by valufied members to a correctional center

The message from the RV organization got to ONITSHA CORRECTIONAL CENTRE. The members met with the inmates and highlighted certain values to them as they undergo their re-orientation. We instituted endowment prizes for best in creativity male and female and also sponsored some students for NABTEB Exam and some renovation work as the Deputy Comptroller informed us when we toured the facility. Cameras and phones were not allowed inside the centre. It was a rewarding experience for our team members as it was their first time entering.

Education event June 2, 2023

Values @ Princewill Memorial High School Onitsha

In the cause to inculcate the spirit of values amongst the society, the RV organization visited Princewill Memorial High School Onitsha and had sessions with the students, on ethics and the spirit of service. Pamphlets, stickers and banners were shared.
